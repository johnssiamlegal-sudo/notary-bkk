Do You Also Need Legalization?

Some countries require documents to be legalized after notarization before they can be accepted overseas. Depending on your destination country, additional authentication may involve:

  • Ministry of Foreign Affairs (Thailand)
  • Foreign Embassy or Consulate
  • Apostille (where applicable outside Thailand)

Our legal team can advise you on the correct procedure for your destination country.
